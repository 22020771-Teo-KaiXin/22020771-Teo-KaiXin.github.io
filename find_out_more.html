Account issuance service:

Issuing a payment account or any service relating to any operation required for operating a payment account. Examples include electronic wallets (e-wallets) or a stored-value card that can be used to pay at various merchants.

Cross-border money transfer service:

Providing inbound or outbound remittance service in Singapore. Merchant acquirers may also be providing a money transfer service if they facilitate the transfer of funds to or from the following entities: Other merchants, The merchant acquirer's payers, The merchant acquirer's payees.

Digital payment token service:

Buying or selling digital payment tokens (DPTs) (commonly known as cryptocurrencies), or providing a platform to allow persons to exchange DPTs.
